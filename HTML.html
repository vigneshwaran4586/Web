In Cyber Security Field Ethical Hacking Playes Major Role

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and data from digital attacks. These attacks aim to access, change, or destroy sensitive information and disrupt normal operations. With the rise of technology, cyber threats have become more sophisticated and widespread. Cybersecurity involves techniques like encryption, firewalls, and secure coding to defend against threats. It plays a vital role in protecting personal, corporate, and government information. Common cyber threats include malware, phishing, ransomware, and hacking. Organizations implement cybersecurity policies and awareness training to reduce risks. Cybersecurity professionals work to detect, prevent, and respond to cyber incidents. Strong cybersecurity practices are essential for maintaining trust in the digital world. As technology evolves, so must the strategies to safeguard our digital assets.
